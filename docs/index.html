About Packsy

Packsy helps you create travel packing checklists, organize items by category, and track packing progress for each trip.

The app is simple, private, lightweight, and designed to work completely offline.

TripsCreate packing lists for different trips.
CategoriesOrganize items into clothes, documents, electronics, toiletries, medicine, and more.
ProgressSee how much of your packing checklist is complete.

Privacy First

Packsy stores your trip and packing checklist data locally on your device. It does not require login, cloud storage, analytics, advertisements, or third-party tracking.
