Privacy Policy

Packsy - Travel Packing Checklist

Last Updated: June 29, 2026

Home Privacy Policy Terms & Conditions

1. Introduction

Packsy is a simple travel packing checklist app designed to help users organize trips and packing items locally on their device.

This Privacy Policy explains how Packsy handles information and is written to clearly describe the app’s local-only data practices.

2. Purpose of the App

Packsy helps users create trips, add packing checklist items, group items into categories, and track packing progress before travel.

3. Information Stored in the App

Packsy stores only the information you choose to enter, including trip names, optional trip notes, packing item names, categories, packed status, favorite status, and app settings such as dark mode.

4. Local Device Storage

All Packsy data is stored locally on your device using local storage. Packsy does not upload your trip or packing checklist information to any server controlled by Packsy.

5. No Account Required

Packsy does not require user registration, login credentials, usernames, passwords, email addresses, phone numbers, or profile accounts.

6. No Personal Data Collection

Packsy does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, address, location, contacts, photos, payment data, passport details, travel document numbers, or government identifiers.

7. User-Entered Content

You control what you type into trip names, notes, and packing items. Please avoid entering sensitive travel document numbers or private personal information unless you understand it is stored locally on your device.

8. Packing Checklist Data

Packing checklist data may include item names, categories, and packed or unpacked status. This information is used only to provide packing checklist functionality inside the app.

9. No Cloud Synchronization

Packsy does not provide cloud synchronization, online backup, account recovery, or remote data restoration. If local app data is deleted, Packsy cannot recover it from a server.

10. Offline Usage

Packsy is designed to work offline. Core functionality such as creating trips, adding checklist items, marking items as packed, and viewing progress does not require internet access.

11. No Analytics

Packsy does not use analytics SDKs or behavioral tracking tools. It does not track screen views, button taps, packing behavior, search activity, or usage frequency.

12. No Advertising

Packsy does not display advertisements and does not include advertising network SDKs, advertising identifiers, personalized ad systems, or marketing trackers.

13. No Third-Party Sharing

Packsy does not sell, rent, trade, disclose, or share your trip or packing checklist data with third parties.

Platform providers such as Google Play may process app download or device-level information under their own policies. That platform-level information is not controlled by Packsy inside the app.

14. Permissions

Packsy is designed to work without sensitive permissions. It does not require access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, calendar, SMS, phone calls, or external files for core functionality.

15. Data Security

Keeping data local reduces privacy risk. Your data is protected by your device security settings, operating system protections, and device encryption where available.

You are responsible for keeping your device secure. Anyone with access to your unlocked device may be able to view or edit your saved packing lists.

16. User Control

You can create, edit, check, uncheck, favorite, and delete trips and checklist items at any time inside the app.

17. Data Deletion

You can delete individual trips or items inside the app. You can remove all locally stored Packsy data by clearing app data in your device settings or uninstalling Packsy.

18. Data Retention

Your packing data remains stored locally until you delete it, clear app data, uninstall the app, or reset your device.

19. Children’s Privacy

Packsy is a general travel organization app and is not specifically directed to children. The app does not knowingly collect children’s personal information.

20. Travel Disclaimer

Packsy is an organization tool only. It cannot guarantee that you packed every required item or travel document. Always check official travel requirements before departure.

21. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect app improvements, legal requirements, or store policy updates.

22. Acceptance

By continuing to use Packsy, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

23. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the developer contact information listed on the app store page or official project website.

Privacy Summary

  • No account required
  • Local device storage only
  • No cloud synchronization
  • No analytics
  • No advertising
  • No third-party tracking
  • No sensitive permissions required
