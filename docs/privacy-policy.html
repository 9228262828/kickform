About KickForm

KickForm helps you create football formations, edit players, choose tactical schemes, and organize your football board in a clean and simple way.

The app is designed for coaches, players, football fans, and anyone who wants a private offline formation tool.

FormationsCreate and save tactical boards.
PlayersEdit names, numbers, positions, and captain status.
OfflineNo account, no backend, no internet required.

Privacy First

KickForm stores formations and player data locally on your device. It does not require login, cloud sync, analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking.
